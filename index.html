About The app

This app represents a simple and convenient approach to perform fruit/vegetable recognition and predict nutrition information of the fruit/vegetable using MobileNet architecture. The datasets employed for analysis are acquired from “Fruit and Vegetables SSM” of image processing challenges. The fruit categories include 29 items which are Apples, Bananas, Broccolis, Carrots, Cauliflowers, Chilies, Coconut, Cucumbers, Custard Apples, Dates, Dragon Fruits, Eggs, Garlics, Grapes, Green Lemons, Jackfruits, Kiwis, Mangos, Okras, Onions, Oranges, Papayas, Peanuts, Pineapples, Pomegranates, Star Fruits, Strawberries, Melons, Mushrooms. Moreover, the experimentations are carried out on various other fruit samples collected from multiple sources.

  • App Type Cross Platform (android and ios)
  • Compatible for 3+
  • Availability All Over the world

Main Purpose

The objective of this project is to help people to find the proper nutrition details from what they eat regularly (fruits and vegetables). One can easily use this software/app to check the amount of each nutrient, how much he/she need to keep himself/herself to fit and sound and the required amount based on his/her Body Mass Index (BMI).
